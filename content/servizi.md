# Our Comprehensive Services: Reset Your Body

At 'Reset Your Body,' we offer a diverse range of treatments and training programs designed to enhance your well-being, promote functional recovery, and help you achieve your fitness goals. Our services are rooted in both massotherapy and kinesiology, providing a holistic approach to physical health.

## Massotherapy Services

Our massage therapies are tailored to address various needs, from deep relaxation and stress relief to targeted pain reduction and improved circulation.

### Total Body Reset

*   **Description:** A complete articular treatment that focuses on realigning and relaxing all major body joints. This intensive session includes an extensive and destressing massage targeting the back, aiming to alleviate tension and restore mobility across your entire body.
*   **Benefits:** Reduces joint stiffness, improves range of motion, relieves chronic back pain, and promotes deep relaxation.

### Total Body Massage

*   **Description:** A classic, comprehensive massage experience that covers your body from head to toe. This treatment is designed to soothe muscles, improve circulation, and induce a state of profound relaxation.
*   **Benefits:** Stress reduction, muscle relaxation, improved blood flow, and a general sense of well-being.

### Gambe Leggere (Light Legs)

*   **Description:** A specialized, complete treatment specifically designed to relieve heaviness and fatigue in the legs. This therapy focuses on improving circulation and reducing fluid retention.
*   **Benefits:** Reduces swelling, alleviates leg fatigue, prevents restless legs syndrome, and enhances overall leg comfort.

### Linfodrenaggio (Lymphatic Drainage)

*   **Description:** A gentle massage technique aimed at stimulating the lymphatic system, which is crucial for detoxification and fluid balance. We offer targeted treatments for specific areas:
    *   **Face Treatment:** Reduces facial puffiness, improves skin radiance, and promotes a clearer complexion.
    *   **Arm Treatment:** Helps reduce swelling and improves circulation in the upper limbs.
    *   **Leg Treatment:** Highly effective for reducing edema, combating cellulite, and alleviating leg heaviness.
*   **Benefits:** Detoxification, reduced swelling, improved immune function, and enhanced skin health.

### Massaggio Decontratturante (Decontracting Massage)

*   **Description:** This therapeutic massage targets muscle contractions and knots, common after physical activity or due to stress. We offer specialized treatments:
    *   **Sport Legs:** Focuses on releasing tension and aiding recovery in the leg muscles of athletes.
    *   **Back/Neck Decontracting:** Addresses stiffness and pain in the back and neck, often caused by poor posture or stress.
*   **Benefits:** Alleviates muscle pain and stiffness, improves muscle flexibility, and speeds up recovery from physical exertion.

### Massaggio Benessere (Wellness Massage)

*   **Description:** Designed for ultimate relaxation and rejuvenation, this massage focuses on overall well-being.
    *   **Face:** A gentle and relaxing massage for the face, relieving tension and promoting a refreshed look.
    *   **Complete Back and Neck:** Targets the entire back and neck area for comprehensive stress and tension relief.
    *   **Total Body:** A full-body experience aimed at harmonizing the mind and body.
*   **Benefits:** Deep relaxation, stress relief, improved mood, and a renewed sense of energy.

## Kinesiology Services

Our kinesiology services focus on movement, posture, and functional recovery, utilizing therapeutic exercises and assessments to improve physical performance and alleviate pain.

### Valutazione Posturale (Postural Assessment)

*   **Description:** A detailed evaluation of your body's posture, identifying imbalances and misalignments. This service includes personalized corrective postural gymnastics to address identified issues.
*   **Benefits:** Corrects poor posture, reduces back and neck pain, prevents future injuries, and improves body awareness.

### Ginnastica Dolce (Gentle Gymnastics)

*   **Description:** Low-impact joint activities suitable for individuals of all ages and fitness levels. This program emphasizes stretch and mobility exercises, designed to improve flexibility, balance, and joint health without strenuous impact.
*   **Benefits:** Enhances flexibility, increases range of motion, improves balance and coordination, and is safe for sensitive joints.

### Valutazione Chinesiologica (Kinesiological Assessment)

*   **Description:** A comprehensive assessment designed for specific functional goals. This service includes:
    *   **Post-Rehabilitation Functional Recovery:** Programs tailored to regain full function after an injury or surgery.
    *   **Recovery of Stability and Strength (Post-Trauma):** Targeted exercises to rebuild core strength and stability, crucial after traumatic events.
*   **Benefits:** Accelerates recovery, restores functional movement, prevents re-injury, and builds resilient strength.
